As if Personal Info wasn't enough

Well I just quickly took a MBTI personality test on 16 Personalities and that said I'm INFJ. Also I hit up my boy Vark at Vark.com and he gave me a questionnaire to answer. That gave me a ranking across four different values. I scored 4/10 for visual learning, 5/10 for aural learning, 2/10 for written learning, and 10/10 for kinesthetic learning. I also completed a quiz titled Interpersonal Skills Self-Assessment which can be found here. That one told me that my interpersonal skills are slightly below average with a score of 48%.


After taking these tests I believe that the MBTI test is accurate, Vark is accurate too, and the third one could be accurate too, although in my arrogance I want to say it's wrong. In the third test, my weakest point was group work. This is largely because I'm not confident about alot of things and so in turn, I generally feel that I don't have much to contribute to conversations. If the conversation turns to something that I'm already confident in, then I can light up a room. In choosing a group, I'd like to be with people who can carry a conversation. I don't want to talk much if I don't have to, and when I do have input, it will be relevant.
